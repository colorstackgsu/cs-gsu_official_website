Welcome to our chapter at Georgia State

Our Mission

The mission of ColorStack is to increase the number of Black, Latinx, and Native American students who pursue and successfully graduate with degrees in Computer Science and other technology-related fields.We provide community support, professional development, and access to opportunities to help underrepresented students navigate the tech industry
